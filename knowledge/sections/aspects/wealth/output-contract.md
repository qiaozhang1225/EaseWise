# Wealth Output Contract

Required fields: `aspect_key`, `title`, `score`, `content`, `risk`, `elements_check`.

`aspect_key` must be `wealth`.

`title` is one direct sentence about wealth.

`score` must equal the locked score.

`content` is one paragraph about income, money flow, cooperation, and mild advice.

`risk` is one paragraph focused on money risks, loss, stuck capital, cooperation complexity, or investment caution.

`elements_check` must include `宫`, `门`, `神`, `星`, `天干/地干`, `特殊组合`, `四害`.

# Health Output Contract

Required fields: `aspect_key`, `title`, `score`, `content`, `risk`, `elements_check`.

`aspect_key` must be `health`.

`title` is one direct sentence about health tendency.

`score` must equal locked score.

`content` is one paragraph about energy, recovery, pressure, and mild advice.

`risk` is one paragraph focused on health risk tendencies. Do not diagnose disease.

`elements_check` must include `宫`, `门`, `神`, `星`, `天干/地干`, `特殊组合`, `四害`.

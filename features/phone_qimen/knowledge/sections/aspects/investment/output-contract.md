# Investment Output Contract

Required fields: `aspect_key`, `title`, `score`, `content`, `risk`, `elements_check`.

`aspect_key` must be `investment`.

`title` is one direct sentence about investment tendency.

`score` must equal locked score.

`content` is one paragraph about investment style and mild advice.

`risk` is one paragraph focused on investment risks. Do not promise returns.

`elements_check` must include `宫`, `门`, `神`, `星`, `天干/地干`, `特殊组合`, `四害`.

Readability rules: `content` and `risk` must prioritize user-verifiable capital pressure, exit, cooperation, lock-up, and decision scenarios over technical terms. If a technical term is used, translate it into a concrete investment manifestation without return promises. Each `elements_check` item must include the professional layer and its real-world meaning.

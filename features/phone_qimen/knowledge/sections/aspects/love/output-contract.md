# Love Output Contract

Required fields: `aspect_key`, `title`, `score`, `content`, `risk`, `elements_check`.

`aspect_key` must be `love`.

`title` is one direct sentence about relationship tendency.

`score` must equal locked score.

`content` is one paragraph about relationship style, verifiable manifestation, and mild advice.

`risk` is one paragraph focused on relationship risks surfaced by element checks.

`elements_check` must include `宫`, `门`, `神`, `星`, `天干/地干`, `特殊组合`, `四害`.
